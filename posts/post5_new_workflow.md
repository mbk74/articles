# Simplify UI

When people talk about AI interfaces, they usually imagine chatbots.

I'm not convinced that's where mobile apps are heading.

Most users don't want to write prompts all day.

They want to get things done.

Maybe the real opportunity is using AI behind the scenes:

* reducing unnecessary navigation,
* prefilling information,
* preventing mistakes,
* adapting complexity to the user.

The most successful AI-powered applications might look surprisingly familiar.

The difference is that the interface adapts to the user, not the other way around.
