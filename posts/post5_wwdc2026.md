# AI may replace navigation before it replaces interfaces

Lots of people expect AI to replace traditional interfaces.

I'm starting to think something else will happen first.

AI may replace navigation.

Users will still interact with lists, cards, forms, and familiar controls.

But the path between screens may become dynamic.

Foundation Models and App Intents make me think we're moving toward a world where applications understand intentions first and navigation second.

Maybe the biggest UI change of the next decade won't be visual.

It will be architectural.
