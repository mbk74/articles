# AI is bringing back invisible UI

People got used to using devices almost silently.  
We scroll, tap, and swipe automatically.

But AI suddenly asks us to do the opposite → talk to computers or write long prompts.

I think this is temporary.

Good AI should move into the background.  
Users should not need to learn complex interfaces or craft perfect prompts.

The future UI may not be hundreds of screens and menus.

It may become a system that understands user intent and helps complete tasks faster and safer.

For example:

- AI could detect suspicious purchases
- wrong product sizes
- risky flight connections

before the payment is made.

Maybe the best AI interface is the one users barely notice.
